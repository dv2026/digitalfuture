Common Questions About Working With Us

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 2-4 hours during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM). For inquiries received outside business hours, we'll respond by the next business day.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer complimentary 30-minute consultations to understand your business needs and provide initial recommendations. This helps us determine if we're the right fit for your digital marketing needs.

What information should I prepare before contacting you?

To make our conversation more productive, we recommend having information about your business goals, target audience, current marketing efforts (if any), budget considerations, and any specific challenges you're facing.

Do you work with businesses outside of India?

Absolutely! We work with clients globally, including the US, UK, Australia, Canada, and Europe. Our digital marketing strategies are tailored to work effectively in any market.

How do you determine which services are right for my business?

During our initial consultation, we'll analyze your business, goals, target audience, and current online presence to recommend the most effective services. Our approach is data-driven and customized to your specific needs.

What's the typical onboarding process?

After our initial consultation and agreement, our onboarding process includes: 1) Comprehensive audit of your current digital presence, 2) Strategy development session, 3) Implementation plan creation, and 4) Regular progress tracking and reporting.
