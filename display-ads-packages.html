What's the difference between display ads and search ads?

Search ads appear when users actively search for keywords on Google. Display ads are shown across websites, apps and videos based on interests, behaviour and audience segments. Display Ads Packages are ideal for brand awareness, remarketing and top-of-funnel reach, while search ads capture bottom-of-funnel intent.

Do I need to provide creative assets for my display campaigns?

If you already have brand guidelines, logos or product photos, we will use them to design your banners. If not, our team will create the complete banner set as part of your Display Ads Package so you get ready-to-launch creatives.

What is remarketing and how does it work in display advertising?

Remarketing lets you show display ads to users who previously visited your website, added products to cart or engaged with your brand. Using tracking pixels, we segment these visitors and show them tailored ads to bring them back and complete their purchase or enquiry.
