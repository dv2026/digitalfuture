Guest Post FAQ

How long does it take to get a response after submission?

Our editorial team typically responds within 3-5 business days. During busy periods, it might take up to 7 days. If you haven't heard back after a week, feel free to follow up via email.

Do you accept previously published content?

No, we only accept 100% original content that has not been published elsewhere, including on your own blog or website. We check all submissions for plagiarism.

Can I include links to my website in the article?

Yes, you can include 1-2 relevant, contextual do-follow links to your website within the article body. Additional links may be included in your author bio.

What happens if my article needs revisions?

If your article needs revisions, our editorial team will provide specific feedback and suggestions. You'll have the opportunity to make the requested changes before publication.

Do you pay for guest posts?

We do not currently offer payment for guest posts. The primary benefits are exposure to our audience, quality backlinks, and establishing yourself as an industry expert.

Can I republish my article on my own blog after it's published on your site?

We ask that you wait at least 30 days after publication on our site before republishing the content elsewhere. When you do republish, please include a canonical link pointing to the original article on our website.
