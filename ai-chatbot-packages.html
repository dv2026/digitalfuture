AI Chatbot FAQs

How long does it take to set up an AI chatbot?

Basic chatbot setup typically takes 1-2 weeks, including strategy, development, and testing. More complex implementations with advanced features and multiple integrations may take 3-4 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase based on your specific requirements.

Can the chatbot integrate with my existing systems?

Yes, our chatbots can integrate with various systems including CRM platforms, payment gateways, booking systems, and more. We assess your existing tech stack during the discovery phase and recommend the best integration approach for your business.

What happens if the chatbot can't answer a question?

Our chatbots are designed with seamless handover capabilities. When a query exceeds the chatbot's capabilities, it can automatically transfer the conversation to a human agent, schedule a callback, or provide alternative contact methods based on your preferences.

How do you measure chatbot success?

We track multiple metrics including conversation completion rate, user satisfaction scores, deflection rate (queries handled without human intervention), lead generation numbers, and cost savings. Our comprehensive reporting provides clear insights into your chatbot's performance and ROI.

Can I update the chatbot content myself?

Yes, we provide an easy-to-use content management system that allows you to update FAQs, responses, and conversation flows without technical knowledge. For more complex updates, our team is available to assist as part of our maintenance packages.

Is there a long-term contract required?

No, all our chatbot packages operate on a month-to-month basis with no long-term contracts. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your service with 30 days' notice. We believe in delivering continuous value to earn your business each month.
