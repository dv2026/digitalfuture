Chatbot Services FAQs

How long does it take to set up a chatbot?

Basic chatbot setup typically takes 1-2 weeks, while more complex implementations with advanced AI training and multiple integrations may take 3-4 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

Do I need technical knowledge to manage the chatbot?

No technical knowledge is required. We provide a user-friendly dashboard where you can easily update responses, add new conversation flows, and monitor performance. For more complex updates, our team handles everything for you.

Can the chatbot integrate with my existing systems?

Yes, our chatbots can integrate with various systems including CRM platforms, email marketing tools, payment gateways, and internal databases. We assess your existing tech stack during the discovery phase to ensure seamless integration.

What happens when the chatbot can't answer a question?

When the chatbot encounters a question it can't answer, it can either escalate to a human agent, collect the user's contact information for follow-up, or direct them to alternative support channels based on your preferences.

How do you measure chatbot success?

We track multiple metrics including conversation completion rate, user satisfaction scores, deflection rate (queries handled without human intervention), lead capture rate, and conversion rates to measure and optimize chatbot performance.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade your package at any time as your needs evolve. We make the transition seamless without interrupting your chatbot service. Many clients start with our Basic package and upgrade as they see the value.
