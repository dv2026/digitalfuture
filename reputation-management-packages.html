Reputation Management FAQs

How long does it take to see results from reputation management?

Initial improvements in review response times and monitoring can be seen within the first month. Significant improvements in ratings and search result changes typically take 3-6 months, depending on the severity of existing reputation issues and the competitiveness of your industry. We provide monthly progress reports to track your reputation improvement.

Can you remove negative reviews?

We cannot guarantee removal of negative reviews, as review platforms have specific policies about content removal. However, we can help you request removal of reviews that violate platform policies, respond professionally to negative reviews to show you care about customer feedback, and implement strategies to push negative content down in search results while promoting positive content.

Do you work with businesses in sensitive industries?

Yes, we have experience working with businesses across various industries, including healthcare, legal, finance, and other sensitive sectors. We understand the unique reputation challenges these industries face and tailor our strategies accordingly while maintaining compliance with industry regulations.

How do you measure reputation management success?

We track a comprehensive set of metrics including overall rating improvements, review volume increases, sentiment analysis, search result changes for brand terms, website traffic from review sites, and conversion rates from reputation-driven traffic. Our reporting includes both platform-specific metrics and business-focused KPIs.

What's included in your crisis management support?

Our crisis management support includes 24/7 monitoring during crises, immediate response strategy development, communication template creation, media relations support if needed, and coordinated efforts across all digital channels to manage and contain the reputation impact of the crisis situation.

Can I pause or cancel my reputation management package?

Yes, all our packages operate on a month-to-month basis with no long-term contracts required. You can pause or cancel your service with 30 days' notice. We believe in earning your business every month through measurable results and excellent service.
