Refund & Return Policy

Our commitment to transparency and customer satisfaction

Our Policy Overview

At DigitallyFuture, we strive to deliver exceptional digital marketing services that exceed your expectations. We understand that circumstances may arise where you need to reconsider your investment. This policy outlines our approach to refunds and returns for our services.

Service Cancellation

You may cancel any ongoing service with a 30-day written notice. Upon cancellation, you will be billed only for services rendered up to the cancellation date.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis for services not yet initiated. Once work has commenced, refunds are typically not provided for completed work.

Contract Terms

All refund requests are subject to the terms outlined in your service agreement. Please review your contract for specific cancellation and refund clauses.

Refund Request Process

1

Submit Request

Contact our support team with your refund request and relevant details.

2

Review & Assessment

Our team will review your request and assess eligibility based on our policy.

3

Resolution

We'll provide a resolution within 7-10 business days of receiving your request.

4

Processing

If approved, refunds are processed within 14 business days to your original payment method.

Service-Specific Policies

SEO Services

Due to the nature of SEO work, refunds are not provided for completed optimization work. However, if you're unsatisfied with our approach, we'll work with you to adjust the strategy or you may cancel future services with proper notice.

PPC & Advertising Services

Refunds are not available for ad spend or management fees for campaigns that have already launched. We can pause campaigns at any time and refund any unused prepaid ad budgets.

Web Development

Web development projects follow a milestone payment structure. Refunds are only considered for work not yet started. Once a milestone is completed and approved, payments for that milestone are non-refundable.

Content Creation

For content creation services, we offer revisions to ensure your satisfaction. If after two rounds of revisions you're still not satisfied, we may issue a partial refund for the specific content pieces in question.

AI & Automation Services

Due to the custom nature of AI solutions, refunds are evaluated based on project stage. Setup fees are non-refundable once implementation has begun.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Third-party costs (ad spend, software licenses, etc.)
  • Completed work that has been delivered and approved
  • Setup or onboarding fees after services have commenced
  • Custom development work that has been completed
  • Services where results are not guaranteed (e.g., SEO rankings)

Dispute Resolution

If you have a concern about our services or billing, we encourage you to contact us directly to resolve the issue. We're committed to working with our clients to find fair solutions. If we cannot resolve a dispute amicably, the terms of your service agreement will govern the resolution process.

Policy Updates

DigitallyFuture reserves the right to update this refund and return policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
