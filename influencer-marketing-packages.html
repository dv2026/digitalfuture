Influencer Marketing FAQs

How much does influencer marketing cost?

Our influencer marketing packages start at just $99/month, making it accessible for businesses of all sizes. The cost varies based on the number of influencers, their reach, and the complexity of campaigns. Micro-influencers typically offer the best ROI for most brands.

How do you select the right influencers for my brand?

We use a comprehensive vetting process that considers audience demographics, engagement rates, content quality, brand alignment, and authenticity. We prioritize influencers whose audience matches your target customers and whose values align with your brand.

What results can I expect from influencer marketing?

Results vary by industry and campaign goals, but typically include increased brand awareness, higher engagement, improved brand trust, and measurable conversions. Most clients see initial results within the first month, with significant impact within 3-6 months of consistent campaigns.

Do you work with micro-influencers or macro-influencers?

We work with both, but typically recommend starting with micro-influencers (1K-50K followers) as they often deliver higher engagement rates and better ROI. Our packages include access to both micro and macro-influencers based on your budget and campaign objectives.

How do you measure the success of influencer campaigns?

We track multiple metrics including engagement rates, reach, website traffic, conversions, and ROI. We also monitor brand sentiment and audience growth. Our comprehensive reporting provides clear insights into campaign performance and business impact.

Can I pause or cancel my influencer marketing package?

Yes, all our packages operate on a month-to-month basis with no long-term contracts. You can pause or cancel your service with 30 days' notice. We believe in earning your business through measurable results and excellent service.
