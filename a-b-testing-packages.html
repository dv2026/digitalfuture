A/B Testing Expertise

What methodology do you use for A/B testing?

We employ a scientific, research-backed approach that begins with comprehensive data analysis and user research. Our methodology focuses on testing strategic hypotheses rather than random changes, ensuring each test is designed to answer specific business questions and generate actionable insights.

How do you determine what to test first?

We prioritize testing opportunities based on potential business impact, implementation complexity, and data confidence. Our analysis includes funnel analysis, user behavior patterns, competitive benchmarking, and qualitative research to identify the highest-impact testing opportunities.

What's your approach to statistical significance?

We require 95% statistical confidence for conclusive results, but also consider practical significance - the real business impact of observed changes. We monitor for false positives, seasonal variations, and external factors that could influence results.

Do you work with low-traffic websites?

Yes, we've developed specialized approaches for lower-traffic sites including Bayesian statistics, sequential testing methods, and longer test durations. We'll assess your situation and recommend the most appropriate testing strategy.

How do you ensure test results are reliable?

We implement multiple validation measures including proper sample sizing, controlled variable testing, cross-device consistency checks, and post-implementation monitoring to ensure observed improvements are genuine and sustainable.

What happens after a test concludes?

We provide comprehensive analysis of results, implement winning variations, document learnings, and integrate insights into your ongoing optimization strategy. Each test conclusion informs future testing priorities and business decisions.
